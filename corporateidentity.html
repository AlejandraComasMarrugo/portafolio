Ce Camilo Foundation

In this institution I have made portfolios of products and services, flyers, brochures, business cards, stikers, pens, notices, advertisements for social networks and much more.

The pieces are made taking into the characteristics of the company, then the identity is built with shapes, typefaces, colors and photographs.

I like to experiment and play with everything at hand, identifying the company's activity must be simple and original to achieve memory and empathy with its users.
